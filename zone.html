About

Feisty Bangalore is all set to welcome another cool hub for you to zone in. Zone by The Park, Infantry Road is based in the busiest hub of the city, with 72 rooms and suites, with each detail crafted carefully, to delight your five senses. Whether it is a business call, or a lazy retreat, we have the secret key to unlock your perfect stay.
